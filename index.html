Our Priorities

  • Fiscal Accountability
  • Transparency and Communication
  • Quality of Life and Economic Growth

Bill Fitzpatrick is a local educator and coach; before teaching he worked as a legislative assistant for Congressman Rick Renzi of Arizona and as a legislative advocate for a national law enforcement officers union.  Bill has worked and continues to work a wide variety of  jobs before, during, and after his transition from politics to education.  Bill is a member of Sacred Heart and is involved with several community organizations. If elected to Oxford Borough Council, Bill will focus on fiscal accountability, increased transparency and communication, economic growth, and quality of life

Michael McMurrough, a Senior Regional Sales Manager for LG Electronics, has spent his entire career in the HVAC industry. Michael, along with his wife, raised a family in the OASD and has volunteered countless hours to various nonprofits in the area utilizing his business skills. Most recently, Michael started an ACL accredited cornhole league in Oxford with his 2 partners, which in addition to growing the sport has been instrumental in raising necessary funding for local organizations and businesses. Additionally, Michael is also a member of The Improved Order of Red Man and the Fraternal Order of Eagles in Oxford. New to the political arena, Michael supports a safe and vibrant downtown, equality and diversity amongst our residents, clear and direct communication to residents and financial accountability from our leaders.
